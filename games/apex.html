About the game:

Basically this is a fast paced shooter in a style of Battle Royal with really cool movement system, that's heavily skill-dependant and has a lot of room for self-improvement at it.

Stats:

  • Play time: 872hrs
  • Total kills: 3k+
  • Favorite weapons: Wingman, Mastiff, R-99
  • Main characters: Wraith, Wattson, Horizon (she's getting nerfed every season Istg)

A message from me about the game itself and my favorite moments from it:

A "review":

I really liked that game when I just started playing it, it was really fun at first, I really like the movement in that game and how dynamic and fast-paced it is (probably the best shooter in these categories imo).

But after a certain season the game started to kinda falling apart. They removed a very good a fast gamemode called arenas, added a lot of shitty micro-transaction items, that devalued previous items, that people worked hard to get them.

My favorite moment of all time:

Probably one of my favorite moments in this game is when I won a game with my friend, got a new level and a lootbox chest for it, opened it and got 2 Heirlooms in a row (basically chance of getting 2 in a row is: 1/25000).
